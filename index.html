How to Fish

Here are basics on how to fish:

In order to catch a fish, you must have a fishing rod ready in your hand. These can be purchased at the Nook's Cranny or crafted using five sticks.

With your fishing rod ready to go, simply press 'A' near a body of water to cast it. If your bobber lands directly in front of a fish shadow's line of sight, the fish will start swimming towards it. Once the fish is close enough, it will swim up to the bobber and touch it.

Be patient; if the fish simply nibbles, reeling in your rod will scare it off. If the fish bites down, however, press 'A' as quickly as possible to reel it in. If you were fast enough, you may have just reeled in a new aquatic friend - or another sea bass.

Comment Pêcher

Voici comment vous pouvez pêcher :

Pour attraper un poisson, vous devez avoir accès a une canne a pêche. Ceux-ci peuvent être achetés a Nook's Cranny ou fabriqués a l'aide de cinq bâtons.

Avec votre canne a pêche prête, appuyez sur près d'un plan d'eau pour le lancer. Si votre bobber tombe directement devant une ombre de poisson, le poisson commencera a nager envers lui. Lorsque le poisson est assez proche, il nagera jusqu'au bobber et le touchera.

Soyez patient; si le poisson grignote simplement, enrouler votre canne l'effraiera. Cependant, si le poisson mord, appuyez sur aussi vite que possible pour l'enrouler. Si vous étiez assez rapide, ça se peut que vous avez attrapé un nouvel ami aquatique - ou un autre bar.

Reaction Test

How the reaction test works:

Try out this reaction test to improve your reaction time and ensure you never let another fish go! Start the test by pressing the 'Start' button below. When starting the test, you'll see a series of up to 4 ' . ' characters appear sequentially; this mimics the fish nibbling on the bobber. Press the button again as soon as the fish bites and '! ! !' appears on the screen. Press it fast enough and you might just catch the fish successfully!

Best Time: 0.420

 

Press the 'Start' Button to begin and press it again once you see '!!!' appear on the screen.
If you press it too soon, it won't count. To retry the test, simply press the button when the results are displayed
